Meetings & Events Protocols
  • • Meetings & events are hosted at a 50% seating capacity
  • • All seating arrangements are spaced 2 meters apart
  • • Meeting rooms are equipped with sanitizer dispensers
  • • Temperature checks is carried out at the entrance
  • • Meeting rooms are disinfected before and after events
  • • Writing materials & stationaries are covered in paper bags
  • • All electronic media equipment such as microphone / pointer / projector are sanitized after usage
  • • Individually packed meal options are available
